Math Club Membership
  Membership is open to all registered UCSD full or part-time students, faculty, and staff interested in Mathematics. It costs nothing to join and there are no dues required. We try to make our events as enjoyable as possible to everyone.

  We encourage members to get together not only during our official meeting times, but also outside of them to promote our desire to create a community of those who enjoy the various aspects and topics of Mathematics.
