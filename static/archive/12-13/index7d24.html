Mailing List

Math Club Mailing List

To join the mailing list, please follow the instructions below.

  1. Direct your web browser to https://mailman.ucsd.edu/mailman/listinfo/math-club-l.
  2. Under "Subscribing to math-club-l", enter the email address at which you wish to receive Math Club emails.
  3. (Optional) Enter your name.
  4. (Optional) Choose a password that you can later use to modify your subscription settings. If you choose not to enter a password, one will be automatically generated for you.
  5. Hit "Subscribe."
