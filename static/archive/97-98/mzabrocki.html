Mike Zabrocki

 

Mike Zabrocki spent 4 years as an undergraduate at UCSD from 1988-1992 and obtained his BA in Math/Computer Science.  He then went on to math graduate school at UCSD and obtained his PhD in 1998 in Combinatorics with his advisor Prof. Adriano Garsia.  He has taught an intermediate algebra course at City College and Calculus and Cryptography (Math 187) at UCSD.  For the 1998-99 acedemic year he will be in Montreal, Canada for a postdoc with UQAM/CRM and University of Montreal doing reseach on Macdonald polynomials.
