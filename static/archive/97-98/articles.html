Articles of Interest

 

Academic Articles/Papers

  • Math Database - Search through journals from 1931-present to locate articles.
  • Math Pages - over 300 articles on number theory, combinatorics, geometry, algebra, calculus, differential equations, probability, statistics, physics, and the history of math.
  • Electronic Mathematical Journals - This page contains a list of mathematics journals with articles on the Web and a list of Web sites for printed journals. It enables you to access information on, or contained in, these journals (e.g., tables of contents of issues, abstracts of papers, actual papers, information about submissions and subscriptions, etc.).
  • Mathematical Preprints and Reports - This page lists Web sites providing electronic mathematics preprints and abstracts.
  • Quantum Lie Algebras- summarizes the current status of the research and provides a bibliography, Mathematica notebooks and animations of quantum root systems.
  • Unified Analysis - papers that explore a new point of view on the calculus, inspired by the realization that a stronger definition of the limit can banish divergence from the logical foundations of the subject.
  • On Euclid's Fifth Postulate - discusses in detail an attempted proof by al-Haytham and Omar Khayyam's resulting criticism. Comments are welcome.

Articles from the Math Advising Office, UCSD Math Department

  • Frequently Asked Questions - some frequently asked questions and answers of undergraduates in taking math.
  • Majors in Math - descriptions of the seven math majors offered at UCSD
  • The Earning Power of Various Majors - median salaries of males aged 35-44 who are in the labor force with a bachelor's (but not higher) degree in various majors.
  • Sample Major Plan in Math - a generalized four-year plan for students majoring in math
  • Career Resources for Mathematics Majors - a list of career options, reference books, videotapes, and websites for math majors.
  • Graduate School Opportunities - a general guide for those considering graduate school in mathematics.
  • Student and Alumni Organizations - general information about Tritons for Mathematics and Mu Alpha Theta (the Math Club).

Other Articles

  • The Most Common Errors in Undergraduate Math - describes the errors that a college professor has seen most frequently in undergraduate mathematics, the likely causes of those errors, and their remedies.
  • Glossary of Mathematical Mistakes - mistakes made over and over by advertisers, the media, reporters, politicians, activists, and in general many non-math people.
  • Math in the Movies - a guide to major motion pictures with scenes of real mathematics.
  • Curious and Useful Math - practical and not-so-practical math tricks.
  • Furman University Mathematical Quotations Server - a collection of mathematical quotations culled from many sources.
  • 1998-1999 Occupational Outlook Handbook - government publication describing the occupational outlook of careers in the field of mathematics.
  • Gateway to Mathematics - a collection of short articles designed to provide an introduction to the areas of modern mathematics, and answers to some common (or not!) questions.
  • Online Math Applications - explains the math involved in investing, music, and science, discusses the history of math, and shows how to use math in your daily life.
  • Putnam Exam Information - general information about the exam and exams and solutions from previous years.
