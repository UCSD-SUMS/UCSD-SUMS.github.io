Lois Yu
Lois Yu is a three time UCSD alumna. In 1993, she graduated from Revelle College with a double major in Chinese Studies and Applied Math (Scientific Programming). Then in 1995, she received an M.S. in GeoScience from SIO/UCSD and an M.S. in Statistics from the UCSD Math Department. Since her June 1995 graduation, Lois has been employed as an analyst at Metron Inc., a scientific consulting company. As an analyst, Lois has contributed to various projects related to Metron's naval warfare simulation, Naval Simulation System (NSS). These projects include developing tracking and motion algorithms, gathering and analyzing statistics for naval studies, and conducting tutorials on the High Level Architecture (HLA) standards which allow simulations to interoperate. If you have questions about Metron and/or career options for UCSD math majors, please feel free to contact Lois.
