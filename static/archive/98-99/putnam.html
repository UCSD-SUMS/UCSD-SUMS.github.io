1998 William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competition

Saturday, December 5, 1998

 The Putnam Competition is a challenging opportunity for you to test your mathematical mettle.  Last year nearly 3000 individuals participated, representing over 400 colleges and universities across Canada and the  United States.

Detailed information and lore can be found at the official website of the competion.


Copies of recent Putnam Problem Sets can be found here.


The competion is open to all UCSD undergraduates.
If you wish to participate, please contact me by email (pfitz@euclid.ucsd.edu) or in person (APM' 5715). The preliminary registration deadline is October 13, 1998  (though changes are possible through mid-November), so don't delay!
