Holding Office

The Math Club as four positions in its Executive Board:

  • President
  • Vice President
  • Treasurer
  • Secretary
Each position has a term of one year and is obtained through election by the general membership during the Spring quarter. Please contact one of the current officers for more information about taking up a position within the Math Club Executive Board.
