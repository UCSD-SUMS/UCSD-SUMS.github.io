Activities

Career Speaker Events: With the help of Jan Bitmead, our fearless Undergraduate Math Advisor, we invite UCSD Mathematics alumni to come back and speak to students about their careers, and how their education in mathematics applies in their current professions.

Annual Math Club Movie: During Week 0 of Fall Quarter, we treat the entire campus to a movie with gratuitous mathematical content at the Price Center Theater.

General Body Meetings: Early in the quarter we all come together to eat pizza, drink Dr Pepper, and talk about and share ideas for our upcoming events.
