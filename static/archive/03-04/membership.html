Math Club Membership

Membership is open to all registered UCSD full or part-time students, faculty, and staff interested in Mathematics. It costs nothing to join and there are no dues required. We try to make our events as free and a enjoyable as possible.

We encourage members to get together not only during our official meeting times, but also outside of them to promote our desire to create a community of those who enjoy the various aspects and topics of Mathematics.

General Body Meetings & Events

During our General Body Meetings, the Math Club Officers present ideas and topics for discussion. These range anywhere between ideas for upcoming events and activities to helpful hints and solutions to the current Games and BrainTeasers question.

For more details about our Math Club Activities, please visit our Activities Page.
