Comments:

  • The department offers seven different majors:

    1. Pure Mathematics
    2. Applied Mathematics
    3. Applied Mathematics - Scientific Programming
    4. Mathematics - Applied Science
    5. Mathematics - Computer Science
    6. Mathematics - Economics (joint major)
    7. Mathematics - Secondary Education.

  • Please consult with the Mathematics Department and refer to the UCSD catalog for detailed information about the different majors and requirements for each.

  • Please consult with academic advisors in your college and refer to the UCSD catalog for detailed information about general education requirements.

  • The total number of courses and the number of upper-division courses required for graduation varies between colleges. Please consult with academic advisors in your college about graduation requirements.
