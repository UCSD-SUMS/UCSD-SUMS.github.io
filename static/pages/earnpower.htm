The Earning Power of Various Majors

 

The following table gives the results of a national survey conducted in 1993, showing median salary of males between 35 and 44 who are in the labor force with a bachelor's (but not higher) degree. The median over all fields was $43,199. Note that the field refers to the undergraduate major, not the current working area.

This data comes from the June issue of "Physics Today", which got it in turn from "Postsecondary Education Opportunity", March 1996.
