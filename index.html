Announcements

This is the web site for the Society for Undergraduate Mathematics Students at University of California, San Diego.

In addition to this website, there is also a Facebook group and a Slack channel that is open is anyone with an @ucsd.edu email address, as well as a standalone event calendar you can bookmark or add to your own calendars.

Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/groups/sumsucsd
Slack: https://ucsdmathematics.slack.com/
Event Calendar: https://tinyurl.com/sums-events

We hold board meetings every Wednesday, from 11am-12pm in AP&M 7412. They are completely open to the public, and we encourage members to attend and have their voices heard!
