Information

This is the website for UCSD SUMS (Society of Undergraduate Mathematics Students).

In addition to this website, there is also a Facebook group and a Slack channel that is open is anyone with an @ucsd.edu email address, as well as a standalone event calendar you can bookmark or add to your own calendars.

Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/groups/sumsucsd
Slack: https://ucsdmathematics.slack.com/
Mailing List: http://sums.ucsd.edu/-signup

We hold business meetings every Monday in AP&M 7218 from 11 a.m. to 12 p.m. during the regular academic quarter. They are completely open to the public, and we encourage members to attend and have their voices heard!
